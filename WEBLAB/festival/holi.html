Holi - Festival of Colors

The pandora box of wholesome fun and Good natured pranks characterise the atmosphere in the colorful festival of holi. This festival is also slowly gaining popularity in the global space. Holi is celebrated by throwing and applying colours on each other,in myriad hues of jubilance.The festival is even played by throwing snd splashing water on each other,via a friendly weaponry of water guns and water ballons.Immerse yourselfin the pinks,greens and reds of holi and forget all your blues for this day .The next morning is celebrated as Rangwali Holi (Dhuleti) where people smear and drench each other with colours. Water guns and water-filled balloons are often used to play and colour each other, with anyone and anyplace being considered fair game to color.
